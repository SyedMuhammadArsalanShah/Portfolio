About Me

A concise academic and professional overview highlighting research focus, teaching philosophy, and applied technical expertise.

Syed Muhammad Arsalan Shah Bukhari (SMASB)

I currently teach at MITI and Aptech Gulshan I & II, and have served as a Visiting Instructor under NAVTTC, delivering training in Data Science, AI, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and software development. I also innovate in Islamic technology, developing AI-powered platforms like the Hidayah Cloud ecosystem, and motivate others through speaking engagements on learning, purpose, and personal growth.

Academic achievements include BS in Islamic Studies with Computer Technology, Aalim Course (Master’s-equivalent in Islamic Studies), Three-year IT Diploma from Eureka, CIT (Web & Application Development) from IBA CIT, and Data Analyst Certification from NED University.

My Data Science skills are honed through hands-on learning on Kaggle, complemented by IBM Data Science Professional Certificate, mentorship under Dr. Zeeshan-ul-Hassan Usmani, and courses from GeeksforGeeks, CodeWithHarry, and Apna College. I actively maintain practical projects on GitHub.
